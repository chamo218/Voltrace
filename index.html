What is Voltrace?

Voltrace is a smart meter that provides precise measurements of voltage, current, electricity consumption, and frequency for homes, with predictive insights on electricity levels. It also includes robust security for web application authentication and supports electricity providers with a prediction module for future electricity generation and power plant performance, making it a versatile solution for both households and the energy industry.

Our Domain

 In today's advanced society, electricity is a fundamental and indispensable resource, supporting a wide array of daily activities. However, with a burgeoning global population and relentless industrial and economic growth, the challenge of balancing electricity supply and demand has become increasingly complex. This underscores the importance of innovative solutions like Voltrace, which enable efficient energy management and prediction, addressing the critical issue of matching electricity supply with ever-increasing demand.

 A notable research gap in the field of electricity management, specifically in the development of smart meters like Voltrace, is the enhancement of predictive capabilities while ensuring robust security. Current systems provide valuable insights into energy consumption, but there's a need to refine predictive accuracy. This involves the development of more advanced algorithms that can analyze a broader range of data points, adapting to changing user behaviors. Simultaneously, there's a growing emphasis on reinforcing security measures, safeguarding user data and protecting against unauthorized access. Bridging this research gap would not only lead to more efficient energy management but also ensure the integrity and privacy of user information in smart metering systems like Voltrace.
